Who am I?

I am the Vice President of the United States and have been proudly serving in my position since July 19th.

I'm a believer in the vision that our founding fathers outlined in the constitution: a small, restricted government within the full control of the people. I'm a believer in freedom of speech as well as freedom of the press. I'm a believer in equality of opportunity for all races, sexes and creeds. I'm a believer in freedom of religion, but also a separation of church and state; and I'm certain that any nation that doesn't follow these mentioned principles will surely fall to tyranny.
