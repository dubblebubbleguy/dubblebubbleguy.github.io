SNN Bought Out

By dubblebubbleguy

July 23rd - 5:10 PM EST

Yesterday at 9:47 PM EST, Spare's News Network was officially bought out by the Right Wing Network for 500$. SNN is now a subsidiary network of RWN, and we have no intention of shutting down SNN. Kaidon Spare, the owner of SNN, has also joined the Right Wing Network as a journalist.

This buyout brings our media competition down to just Lander's News Network, which is currently a subscriber only network. All free media in Lander's United States is now under the RWN umbrella. As the network manager of RWN, I intend to take my responsibility seriously and to strive even more to bring accurate and comprehensive articles to our readers. I wish the best of luck to our two new journalists, Nicholas Richardson and Kaidon Spare, and I hope that they will be a valuable addition to our network.
